## Motivation

I want this site to serve both as a public personal homepage and as a long-term archive for research notes, technical writing, project logs, and paper reading records.

## Design Principles

- Keep content in Markdown.
- Make research, notes, blog posts, and projects easy to browse.
- Use tags and a knowledge graph to connect related ideas.
- Keep the site deployable with GitHub Pages.

## Next Steps

The next step is to import selected notes from Yuque and selected paper notes from Zotero, while keeping the public site curated rather than dumping everything at once.
